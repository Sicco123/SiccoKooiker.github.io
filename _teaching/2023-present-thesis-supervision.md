Supervising Bachelor and Master students during their thesis projects in Econometrics, Data Science, and related fields. Topics focus on machine learning, time series forecasting, and econometric methods.

Supervision Areas
======
* Machine learning applications in economics
* Time series econometrics
* Forecasting methods
* Macroeconometrics
* Financial econometrics

Approach
======
* Regular one-on-one meetings
* Guidance on methodology and research design
* Support in literature review and empirical analysis
* Feedback on writing and presentation
